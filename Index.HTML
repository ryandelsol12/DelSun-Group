Our Mission

A Healthier Ocean
is Possible

More than 70% of our planet is ocean — yet less than 3% is formally protected. At Del Sun, we believe the fate of the ocean and the fate of humanity are inseparable.

We work alongside marine scientists, coastal communities, and policymakers to reverse decades of damage and restore the biodiversity that sustains all life on Earth.

Our Approach

Science-Led.
Community-Driven.

Every program follows a rigorous four-step framework — ensuring donor funds create lasting, measurable impact in the ecosystems that need it most.

01

Research & Assessment

Our scientists establish baseline ecosystem data before any intervention, building the evidence base for targeted, effective action.

02

Community Partnership

We co-design programs with local fishing communities, governments, and NGOs to ensure cultural fit and long-term ownership.

03

Targeted Intervention

Science-backed initiatives are deployed with clear milestones, timelines, and measurable outcomes tied to ecological recovery.

04

Long-term Monitoring

We track outcomes for years after each initiative, publishing open-access data and adjusting strategy based on results.
